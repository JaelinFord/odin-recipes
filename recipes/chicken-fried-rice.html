Chicken Fried Rice

Description

This is a easy to follow Chicken Fried Rice Recipe. This easy Chicken Fried Rice is loaded Chicken and Veggies and only takes 20 minutes to make! A much better and healthier option than takeout!

Ingredients

  • 2.5 - 3 cups of rice (cooked and chilled the day before for best results)
  • 1.5 - 2 lbs of chicken breast cut into bite size pieces
  • 3 tsp Sesame Oil
  • 3 tsp Vegetable oil
  • 2 cups mixed frozen veggies, Corn, Peas, and Carrots
  • 1/4 cup green Onions
  • 1 tbsp minced Garlic
  • 3 large Eggs, lightly scrambled
  • 2 Tbsp Soy Sauce (add more if needed) to taste

Steps

  1. In a large non-stick wok or skillet, heat 1 1/2 tsp sesame oil and 1 1/2 tsp of the vegetable oil over medium-high heat.
  2. Add chicken pieces, season lightly with salt and pepper and saute until cooked through, about 5 - 6 minutes.
  3. Transfer chicken to a plate or a piece of foil and set aside.
  4. Return skillet to medium-high heat, add remaining 1 1/2 tsp sesame oil and 1 1/2 tsp vegetable oil.
  5. Add corn, peas, and carrots blend and green onions and saute 1 minute, then add garlic and saute 1 minute longer.
  6. Push veggies to edges of pan.
  7. Add eggs in center and cook and scramble until just set.
  8. Return chicken to skillet along with rice.
  9. Add in soy sauce and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  10. Toss everything together and serve warm with Sriracha to taste if desired.
